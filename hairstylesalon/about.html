the SR-71 "Blackbird"

The SR-71 was a un detectable spy plane made to fly into enemy territory and get intel on what was happening. The SR-71 had a trick up its sleeve and that trick was how fast it could fly, it could fly 3 times the speed of sound and was faster than a bullet ,and what was even better was that it was faster than anything the USSR had to offer in both aircraft and missle

during the bulid up of the cold war, both Russia and America were trying to grt the advantage over each other. In 1956 the u-2 "dragonlady" entered service as the first ever spy plane and it was meant to fly into russia and take pictures deep into russian territory and at a staggering 70,000 feet it was untouchable. But one issue with the u-2 was how vulnerable it was, and with no countermeasurs and only a single non-afterburning engine it was only a matter of time until russia developed better surface to air missles that could reach the u-2
